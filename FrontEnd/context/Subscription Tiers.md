# Subscription Tiers

Studyond is free for [[Students]], [[Universities]], and [[Supervisors]]. Revenue comes from [[Companies]] who pay a subscription to post topics, source talent, and access research capacity. All prices are charged upfront, excluding VAT.

## Basic -- CHF 4,900 / year

Entry-level employer presence and qualified inbound:

- Post up to 2 jobs (no time limit)
- 2 users (Owner) + unlimited [[Experts|Expert]] users
- Unlimited [[Topics]] and thesis-internships
- Company profile and employer branding
- AI Topic Agent ([[Expert AI Agent]])
- Active [[Matching Engine|matching algorithm]]
- Structured role and skill tagging
- Lightweight [[Application Management]]
- ATS integration and compatibility
- Email support and guided onboarding

## Professional -- CHF 18,900 / year (Recommended)

Targeted campus sourcing and shortlists. Everything in Basic, plus:

- Post up to 10 jobs (no time limit)
- 5 users (Owner) + unlimited [[Experts|Expert]] users
- Active sourcing (direct access and outreach to relevant profiles)
- 365-day prioritized employer branding at 20+ Swiss universities
- Dedicated 1:1 support and customer success manager
- [[Study Programs|Study program]] targeting
- Shortlist delivery support
- Structured activation workshop
- Academic webinars and engagement formats

## Enterprise -- Custom, from CHF 48,200 / year

Strategic talent infrastructure. Everything in Professional, plus:

- Modular job packages (capacity-based)
- Custom user setup (unlimited users)
- Co-creation and advisory modules
- Official Innovation Partner label
- Exclusive academic visibility and positioning
- Multi-year pipeline planning and forecasting
- Governance and quarterly outcome reviews
- Single Sign-On (SSO)

## Individual Accounts (Free)

All individual accounts are free with no trial period: [[Students]] (topic discovery, career opportunities), [[Supervisors]] (research publishing, student connection), and [[Experts]] (topic browsing, talent pipeline exploration). See [[Company Value Proposition - Talent]] and [[Company Value Proposition - Innovation]] for why companies pay.
