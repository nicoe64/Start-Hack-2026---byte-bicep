# Mid-Stage Students

Students whose thesis is already in progress. They have a topic but need execution support -- primarily [[Interview Partners]], [[Data Access]], and [[Mentor and Feedback]]. They may have found a topic elsewhere but need connections to complete their research.

## Profile

Focused, task-oriented, and time-pressured. Their narrative arc is Need, Discovery, Validation, Outreach. They know what they are looking for and need specific connections, not broad exploration. Cold LinkedIn outreach feels uncomfortable and has low response rates.

## Key Pain Point

Isolation during the [[Execution Stage]]. Their thesis is underway, they have research questions, but they lack connections to relevant professionals. The platform connects them with [[Experts]] who have opted in to support student research -- a structured alternative to cold outreach.

## What They Need

Quick access to relevant experts willing to participate in thesis interviews. Field and expertise filtering, opt-in status indicators ("willing to support student research"), and message templates for professional outreach. They need efficiency, not exploration.

## Trust Triggers

- Experts have opted in and want to help students
- Field relevance -- experts match their specific research domain
- University endorsement of the platform
- Structured process for requesting and conducting interviews

## Tone

Efficient, practical, action-oriented. Respect their time. Assume motivation and focus. Treat them as researchers, not novices.
