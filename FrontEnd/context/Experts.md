# Experts

Experts are industry professionals from [[Companies]] who participate on the Studyond platform. They are the company-side counterpart to [[Supervisors]], bridging the gap between business challenges and academic research.

## Platform Profile

- **Title** -- role at the company (e.g., "Head of Innovation", "Senior Data Scientist")
- **Company** -- organizational affiliation via `companyId`
- **Interview availability** -- `offerInterviews` flag indicating willingness to serve as [[Interview Partners]] for student research
- **Objectives** -- what they seek: `recruiting`, `fresh_insights`, `research_collaboration`, `education_collaboration`, `brand_visibility`
- **Fields** -- professional domains they work in (linked to [[Fields]])

## What Experts Do on Studyond

Experts post [[Topics]] that translate business challenges into thesis opportunities, review student applications through [[Application Management]], collaborate on [[Thesis Projects]], and communicate with [[Students]] via [[Direct Messaging]]. The [[Expert AI Agent]] helps them transform vague business needs into structured research questions.

## Company Team Structure

Under [[Subscription Tiers]], companies get a set number of Owner users plus unlimited Expert users. This means every relevant professional at a company can participate on the platform without additional cost. Experts appear on the company profile page and in the people directory.
