# Company Value Proposition - Innovation

Student theses function as low-risk innovation sprints. Define a question, get matched with a motivated student and academic supervisor, and receive decision-ready evidence in 4-6 months. No hiring, no consultants, no long-term commitment.

## One-Liner

"Explore, validate, de-risk -- with academic rigor and at a fraction of consulting cost."

## ROI Framing

CHF 18,900/year for up to 10 exploration sprints vs. CHF 50-150K for a single consultant engagement. If ONE thesis replaces ONE pilot or kills ONE bad idea early, the investment pays for itself. 41% of firms in [[University-Industry Collaboration Research|UIC]] report concrete innovation outcomes (Alpaydin & Fitjar, 2024, n=232). Education-oriented collaborations produce: 24% product innovation, 14% process innovation, 10% organizational innovation.

## Evidence

Thesis collaborations build [[Absorptive Capacity]] -- firms learn faster, evaluate ideas better, reduce uncertainty (Cohen & Levinthal, 1990). Four proximity benefits from UIC: cognitive, organizational, institutional, social (Boschma, 2005). Innovation is path-dependent: firms investing early in university relationships extract more value later (Bishop et al., 2011; Perkmann et al., 2021). Even without immediate product outputs, collaborations increase cognitive and organizational proximity to [[Universities]].

## What Innovation Teams Can Do

- Post thesis and research [[Topics]] to test ideas, validate assumptions, or explore new markets
- AI Topic Agent helps transform business challenges into structured research questions
- Get matched with qualified [[Students]] and academic [[Supervisors]] through the [[Matching Engine]]
- Track innovation signals -- see which [[Topics]] attract interest and from which [[Universities]]
- Use theses as exploration sprints -- scope a question, get evidence, make kill/continue/scale decisions

## Separation from Talent

Do NOT mix innovation and talent messaging. [[Innovation Managers]] have different goals, metrics, and objections than TA/HR buyers. See [[Company Value Proposition - Talent]] for the talent value path.
