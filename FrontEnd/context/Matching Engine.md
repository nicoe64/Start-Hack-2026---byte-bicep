# Matching Engine

The Matching Engine is Studyond's AI-powered system that connects [[Students]] with relevant [[Topics]] based on structured profile data. Co-developed with Innosuisse, it is the primary intelligence layer driving the platform's value for all three sides of the [[Three-Sided Marketplace]].

## How It Works

The engine matches based on multiple signals:

- **University** -- institutional context and geographic relevance
- **Field of study** -- alignment between student [[Fields]] and topic [[Fields]]
- **Skills** -- specific competencies listed on student profiles
- **Educational level** -- degree type (Bachelor, Master, PhD) matching topic requirements

These signals feed both direct match suggestions and the [[Student AI Agent]]'s topic recommendations.

## Multi-Sided Matching

The Matching Engine operates across all platform stakeholders:

- **Students to Topics** -- surfaces the most relevant thesis topics and job listings based on a student's profile
- **Supervisors to Opportunities** -- connects [[Supervisors]] with research opportunities that align with their interests
- **Companies to Candidates** -- helps [[Companies]] identify qualified students who match their topic requirements

## Role in the Platform

The Matching Engine is currently the primary AI feature alongside the [[Student AI Agent]] and [[Expert AI Agent]]. It powers the "Hire by skills, not by chance" value proposition -- ensuring [[Companies]] only spend time with graduates who are intrinsically motivated and genuinely interested in their business.
