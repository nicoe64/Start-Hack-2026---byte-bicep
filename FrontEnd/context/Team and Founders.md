# Team and Founders

The [[Studyond]] team combines deep academic experience, enterprise software expertise, and proven entrepreneurial track records. Multiple team members have supervised 50+ theses, giving them first-hand understanding of the problem Studyond solves.

## Core Team

**Dr. Philipp Wetzel -- CEO.** University of St. Gallen. Co-founded Calculum (HSG spin-off, AI-powered payment optimization). PhD in Supply Chain Financing with publications in top-tier journals. Supervised 50+ theses.

**Micha Brugger -- Brand & Research.** CEMS, University of St. Gallen. Creator of Biddit (acquired by SHSG, used by ~80% of HSG students). PhD candidate in Information Systems with A-Journal publication, focusing on GenAI in education. Open source creator with 250,000+ downloads.

**Romeo Arpagaus -- Head of Product.** University of St. Gallen. Co-founded Umantis (HR/SaaS platform, sold to HAUFE Group 2012, later to Abacus 2023). 20+ years product management experience.

**George Tsopouridis -- CTO.** 15+ years enterprise software architecture. Cloud-native expert. Previous: LG Electronics, Sunrise, Umantis. AWS Certified Solutions Architect.

**Dr. Alexandra Allgaier -- Academic Partnerships.** University of St. Gallen. PhD in Accounting. Member Swiss GAAP FER. Supervised 50+ theses.

**Alex Cibotari -- Senior Developer.** Led team of 32 developers at Sunrise. Cloud and microservices expert. 19 years full-stack experience.

**Daniel Nydegger -- Corporate Partnerships.** ETH Zurich. 10+ years in financial services sales and advisory (B2B & B2C).

**Florence Hafner -- Accounting.** HSO Business School. 5+ years in university administration. Managing Director at two HSG institutes.
