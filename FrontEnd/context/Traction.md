# Traction

[[Studyond]] has established strong early traction across platform adoption, university partnerships, and academic validation.

## Platform Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| [[Students]] | 3,300+ |
| Professors ([[Supervisors]]) | 230+ |
| [[Companies]] | 185+ |
| [[Topics]] | 7,500+ |

Revenue comes entirely from [[Companies]] through [[Subscription Tiers]]. The platform is free for [[Students]] and [[Universities]].

## University Reach

- Students from 20+ Swiss [[Universities]] on the platform
- 7 study program co-creations launched Q1 2026 (first batch)
- Co-creation partners include HSG and BFH (Bern, university-wide rollout 2026)
- Pipeline of additional programs across German- and French-speaking Switzerland

## Academic Demand Validation

- **Switzerland survey:** 493 study program leaders completed a 30+ minute survey (51.2% response rate)
- **Austria survey:** 302 study program leaders (ongoing, supporting [[DACH Expansion]])

## Thesis Impact Summit 2025

Hosted at HSG. Over 215 decision-makers from 19 Swiss universities, including [[Program Directors]], professors, and education leaders. Joined by representatives from leading [[Companies]].

## Backing

[[Studyond]] is co-developed with Innosuisse (Switzerland's federal innovation agency), in partnership with ETH Zurich, HSG, OST, and the Swiss Employers Association. Paying clients include Big 4 and Fortune 500 companies.
