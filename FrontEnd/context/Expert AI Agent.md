# Expert AI Agent

The Expert AI Agent is Studyond's conversational AI feature for [[Experts]]. It helps industry professionals from [[Companies]] transform business challenges into structured research questions and publishable [[Topics]].

## What It Does

The agent guides company experts through topic creation via dialogue. It helps frame the research question, determine what data is available, identify which degree level fits (Bachelor, Master, PhD), and shape the topic into a format that attracts the right [[Students]]. The result is a well-structured thesis topic that can be published directly through the conversation.

## How It Works

Using the same Vercel AI SDK and Anthropic models as the [[Student AI Agent]], the Expert AI Agent provides a chat-based interface where professionals describe their business challenge in natural language. The agent asks clarifying questions, suggests structuring approaches, and can create [[Topics]] directly from the conversation flow.

## Current Scope

The agent focuses on topic creation -- helping [[Experts]] move from a vague business need to a concrete thesis brief. It does not currently assist with candidate evaluation, [[Thesis Projects]] management, or ongoing collaboration coordination.
