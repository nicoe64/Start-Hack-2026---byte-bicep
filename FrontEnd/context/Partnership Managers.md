# Partnership Managers

Operational staff at [[Universities]] who manage industry connections. They handle the logistics of connecting faculty with [[Companies]] and often do this manually through email, spreadsheets, and one-off introductions. [[Studyond]] replaces their manual coordination with structured platform access.

## Profile

Their narrative arc is Manual Burden, Automation Discovery, Workload Validation, Operational Adoption. "I spend hours emailing companies and faculty to coordinate connections" becomes "A platform that automates this matching and coordination?" then "This actually reduces my workload instead of adding to it." They are day-to-day users who coordinate between companies and university departments.

## What They Need

Proof that the platform reduces manual workload while expanding reach. Key capabilities: companies post topics directly (no manual brokering), students apply independently (partnership manager is not the bottleneck), and automated reporting dashboards replace spreadsheets for leadership.

## Operational Efficiency

The core value for partnership managers is efficiency. Before Studyond: manually email companies for topics, coordinate student-company introductions one-by-one, build spreadsheets for leadership reporting, and watch relationships break when the partnership manager changes roles. After: companies post directly, students self-serve, dashboards auto-generate, and the platform provides continuity.

## Trust Triggers

- Clear how the platform reduces manual work (matching, coordination, reporting)
- Easy to invite existing industry partners onto the platform
- Automated reporting dashboard for leadership
- Peer partnership managers at other universities using it successfully

## Key Objections

"This looks like MORE work, not less" -- initial onboarding takes 2-3 hours; after that, the platform handles matching, coordination, and reporting. "Will our industry partners actually use a platform?" -- companies post topics once per semester; it is less work than coordinating via email.
