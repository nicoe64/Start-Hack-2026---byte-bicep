# Student Value Proposition

Free access to real thesis [[Topics]] from real [[Companies]], AI-powered [[Matching Engine|matching]] to your field and interests, and direct connections to [[Interview Partners|expert interview partners]] -- all through your university.

## One-Liner

"Find your thesis topic in 20 minutes. Real topics from real companies. Free, no strings."

## Emotional Hook

The student arrives anxious: "What if I pick the wrong topic and waste 6 months?" [[Studyond]] transforms fear of the wrong topic into discovery of relevant options. The arc moves from anxiety ("I don't know where to start") to relief ("There are actually good options") to confidence ("I can see which ones match my field") to action ("I'm signing up and applying").

## How It Works

Sign up with a university email for free. Browse and filter thesis topics from 185+ [[Companies]] across industries. The [[Student AI Agent]] suggests the most relevant [[Topics]] for your profile. Apply directly with one click. Need interview partners for qualitative research? Browse [[Experts]] by domain and connect directly through the platform. Time to first value: browse matching topics within minutes of signup.

## Differentiators

| Alternative | Why Studyond Wins |
|---|---|
| Google | Random results, no curation vs. curated topics matched to your field |
| Cold-emailing professors | Hit-or-miss, slow vs. structured [[Matching Engine|matching]] |
| Career fairs | Generic, brief interactions vs. 6-month collaboration through [[Thesis as Work Sample]] |
| LinkedIn | Overwhelming, not thesis-focused vs. purpose-built for thesis discovery |
| Personal network | Limited reach vs. access to [[Companies]] and [[Experts]] beyond your circle |

## For PhD Candidates

[[PhD Candidates]] have fundamentally different needs: longer timelines, deeper research scope, dual career orientation. Studyond serves them through a distinct value path. Company [[Topics]] serve as research contexts for dissertations, not just thesis topics. The platform connects [[PhD Candidates]] with [[Companies]] for Innosuisse projects and research collaborations.

## Key Messages

- **Free, no strings.** Revenue comes from [[Companies]], not [[Students]].
- **Real topics from real companies.** These are not made-up exercises.
- **University-endorsed.** [[Program Directors]] integrate Studyond into thesis modules via [[Curriculum-Embedded Distribution]].
- **Your thesis is your career capital.** A thesis completed with a company is a 6-month [[Work Sample Tests|work sample]] -- the strongest predictor of job performance.
