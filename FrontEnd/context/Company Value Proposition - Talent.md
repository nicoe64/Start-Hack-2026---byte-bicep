# Company Value Proposition - Talent

Year-round access to motivated students from 1,680+ [[Study Programs]] across 20+ Swiss [[Universities]] during their final semester, when they are making career decisions. Assess candidates through real thesis work, not CVs.

## One-Liner

"Hire by work sample, not by CV. 60% lower cost-per-hire than LinkedIn Recruiter."

## ROI Framing

CHF 15,000 for a career fair yields roughly 3 candidate conversations. CHF 4,900/year on [[Studyond]] yields a continuous pipeline to 1,680+ [[Study Programs]]. A thesis is a 6-month [[Work Sample Tests|work-sample]] evaluation -- the strongest predictor of future job performance per the [[Selection Validity Hierarchy]]. Cost per hire via Studyond Pro: approximately CHF 6,300 (if 30% of thesis students convert). Compare: LinkedIn Recruiter CHF 15K+, career fair CHF 20K per event, miss-hire CHF 20K+.

## Evidence

- [[Work Sample Tests]] are the strongest performance predictor (r=.54) per Schmidt & Hunter (1998)
- GenAI inflates CV surface quality, making candidates less distinguishable (see [[CV Signal Degradation]])
- [[Students]] are invisible on professional networks until weeks before graduation
- [[Skills-Based Hiring]] outperforms pedigree-based hiring in fast-changing roles (McKinsey, 2021)
- Students in real-world projects show stronger professional readiness (Esangbedo et al., 2024)

## What TA Teams Can Do

- Post young talent roles with structured skill tagging
- Connect roles to [[Thesis Projects]] -- evaluate candidates through thesis work before making offers
- Active sourcing (Pro+) -- browse and reach out to relevant student profiles directly
- [[Application Management]] -- review applications through a lightweight system
- Year-round visibility via company profile across 20+ Swiss [[Universities]]

## Separation from Innovation

Do NOT mix talent and innovation messaging. [[HR and Talent Acquisition]] buyers have different goals, metrics, and objections than innovation buyers. See [[Company Value Proposition - Innovation]] for the innovation value path.
