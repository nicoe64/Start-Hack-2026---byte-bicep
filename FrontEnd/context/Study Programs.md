# Study Programs

Study Programs are academic degree programs at [[Universities]], and they function as first-class entities on the Studyond platform. They are the key lever for distribution: one [[Program Directors|program director]] embedding Studyond unlocks an entire student cohort.

## Key Properties

- **Name** -- e.g., "MSc Computer Science", "BSc Business Administration"
- **Degree type** -- BSc, MSc, or PhD
- **University** -- the parent [[Universities|institution]]
- **Description** -- program context useful for matching
- **Logo and branding** -- visual identity that travels with every student interaction

## Platform Scale

1,680+ study programs across 20+ Swiss universities are accessible through Studyond. Programs appear on student profiles (visible to [[Companies]] reviewing candidates), in the student onboarding flow (degree-filtered program search), and throughout the platform wherever students are displayed.

## Distribution Mechanism

[[Program Directors]] control curricula, students, and industry exposure. A single program director recommending Studyond during a thesis module kickoff (a 10-minute action) unlocks the entire cohort. Students sign up independently, select their program during onboarding, and immediately access 7,500+ [[Topics]]. No IT integration, no procurement, no budget required. See [[Curriculum-Embedded Distribution]] for the full strategy.

## Program Brand Propagation

Every [[Students|student]] who selects a study program during onboarding becomes an ambassador for that program across the platform. [[Companies]] reviewing candidates see the program name and university on every student card and profile -- building the program's industry reputation passively over time.
