# Supervisors

Supervisors are faculty members at [[Universities]] who supervise thesis work. On the Studyond platform, they operate as a user role with research interests, objectives, and [[Fields|field]] associations.

## Platform Profile

- **Title** -- academic title (e.g., "Prof. Dr.")
- **University** -- institutional affiliation
- **Research interests** -- specific areas of expertise (e.g., "NLP", "knowledge graphs", "information retrieval")
- **Objectives** -- what they seek on the platform: `student_matching`, `research_collaboration`, `network_expansion`, `funding_access`, `project_management`
- **Fields** -- academic domains they work in

## What Supervisors Do on Studyond

Supervisors post [[Topics]] (research questions and thesis opportunities), get matched with [[Students]] and [[Companies]] through the [[Matching Engine]], and provide academic oversight on [[Thesis Projects]]. They appear on university profile pages and in the people directory, where [[Companies]] can discover potential academic partners.

## Academic Governance Role

Thesis supervision is the academic quality control layer. Academic supervision authority is non-negotiable -- [[Universities]] retain full control over academic standards per [[Academic Governance]]. Studyond's governance-first design ensures supervisors maintain institutional autonomy; the platform facilitates connections but never overrides academic decision-making.
