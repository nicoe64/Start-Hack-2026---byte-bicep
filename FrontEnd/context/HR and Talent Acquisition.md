# HR and Talent Acquisition

The primary buyer for the talent use case within [[Companies]]. HR and Talent Acquisition leads own the recruiting pipeline and are measured on cost-per-hire, time-to-fill, and candidate quality. They value thesis collaboration as a 6-month work-sample evaluation.

## Competitive Comparison

Traditional channels deliver diminishing returns. LinkedIn Recruiter costs CHF 10K+ per year with low signal quality. Career fairs run CHF 15-20K per event with limited reach and no extended evaluation. Recruiting agencies charge per-placement premiums. [[Studyond]] offers a structurally different channel: the [[Subscription Tiers|Basic tier at CHF 4,900/year]] delivers continuous pipeline access through thesis-based evaluation.

## ROI Case

Pro tier (CHF 18,900/year) includes 10+ thesis collaborations. If 30% convert to hires, that yields 3+ hires per year at approximately CHF 6,300 each -- 60% less than LinkedIn Recruiter's estimated CHF 15K per hire. The evaluation depth is incomparable: 3-6 months of real work versus 3-5 hours of interviews.

## Narrative Arc

Cost Pressure, Alternative Discovery, ROI Validation, Pilot. "Traditional recruiting is expensive and yields mediocre candidates" becomes "The cost per qualified candidate is 60% less than LinkedIn Recruiter" and "Let me run a pilot and compare results."

## Key Objection

"We already work with universities through career fairs" -- career fairs give visibility but no extended evaluation. Thesis collaboration is a 3-6 month [[Thesis as Work Sample|work sample]]. You see how candidates think, execute, and communicate before making an offer.
