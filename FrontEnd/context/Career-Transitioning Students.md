# Career-Transitioning Students

Students finishing their studies and actively entering the job market. The thesis is both an academic milestone and a career launchpad. They are highly motivated by company [[Topics]] that could lead to employment.

## Profile

These students face the "what now?" question. Their narrative arc is Uncertainty, Opportunity, Connection, Application. They may be returning [[Studyond]] users who completed a thesis with a company, or new visitors discovering opportunities. Their emotional state is hopeful but anxious.

## Thesis as Career Capital

Career-transitioning students are the most responsive to [[Thesis as Work Sample]] messaging. A thesis project is a 6-month demonstration of capabilities -- how they think, execute, and communicate. Companies that collaborate with a student on a thesis already know their work quality, making the hiring conversation far stronger than a cold application.

## What They Need

Job opportunities from companies that value university talent. The [[Company Partner]] relationship means companies on the platform specifically seek graduates who have demonstrated real skills. If a student did a thesis through Studyond, they already have a foot in the door.

## Trust Triggers

- Thesis-to-hire track record -- evidence that companies hire students they have worked with
- Company quality -- recognizable employers, not just any jobs
- Relevant roles matching their field and skills
- Personalized advantage if they completed a thesis via the platform

## Tone

Encouraging, empowering, professional but warm. Validate the transition moment. Emphasize readiness and capability. Reduce anxiety: "companies here are looking for people like you."
