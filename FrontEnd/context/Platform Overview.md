# Platform Overview

Studyond is a [[Three-Sided Marketplace]] that connects [[Students]], [[Companies]], and [[Universities]] around real-world thesis topics, research projects, and talent sourcing. The platform is free for students and universities; revenue comes from company subscriptions.

## How It Works for Students

[[Students]] sign up free with a university email, browse [[Topics]] from companies across Switzerland filtered by field, industry, and degree level, and apply directly. The [[Matching Engine]] and [[Student AI Agent]] surface personalized topic suggestions based on the student's profile, field, and interests. Students can also connect with [[Experts]] for interviews, mentoring, and thesis support via [[Direct Messaging]].

## How It Works for Companies

[[Companies]] register and verify with a company email, then publish topic briefs describing their challenges. The [[Expert AI Agent]] helps professionals transform business challenges into structured research questions. Companies receive AI-supported candidate matches through the [[Matching Engine]], review applications via [[Application Management]], and connect with the best-fit students. They can also post jobs (internships, working student, graduate roles) alongside thesis topics.

## How It Works for Universities

[[Universities]] register with an institutional email, publish or join research topics aligned with teaching and research priorities, and get matched with relevant company and expert partners. [[Program Directors]] can embed Studyond into thesis modules so students access it as part of coursework -- one recommendation unlocks an entire cohort. See [[Curriculum-Embedded Distribution]].

## Platform Scale

- 7,500+ [[Topics]]
- 200+ [[Companies]]
- 44+ Swiss universities
- 1,680+ [[Study Programs]]

## Existing Features

Core capabilities include topic discovery, AI matching via the [[Matching Engine]], [[Application Management]], [[Direct Messaging]], company and university directories, bookmarking, notifications, and reporting analytics. AI features include the [[Student AI Agent]] (topic discovery chat), [[Expert AI Agent]] (guided topic creation), and profile checking. See [[Data Model]] for entity structure, [[Tech Stack]] for implementation details, and [[Subscription Tiers]] for the commercial model.
