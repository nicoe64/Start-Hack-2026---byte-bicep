# University Value Proposition

Systematic, scalable industry linkage at zero cost. No IT integration, no procurement. [[Studyond]] embeds directly into thesis modules, giving [[Program Directors]] real-world [[Topics]] for students and giving [[Researchers]] structured access to industry partners -- all while the university retains full [[Academic Governance|academic control]].

## One-Liner

"Structured industry partnerships at scale. Free. Academic control stays with you."

## Value for Program Directors

[[Program Directors]] get real-world thesis [[Topics]] from industry partners matched to their [[Study Programs]]. [[Curriculum-Embedded Distribution]] means Studyond integrates into existing thesis modules -- students use it as part of their coursework. Measurable industry engagement per cohort: track which [[Companies]] engage with which programs, how many [[Students]] connect, and what outcomes result. Stronger employability outcomes: graduates who complete industry-sourced [[Thesis Projects]] have demonstrable work experience.

## Value for Researchers

[[Researchers]] get structured access to industry partners for applied research projects, [[PhD Candidates]] recruitment, and Innosuisse innovation projects. Industry partnerships without brokerage overhead -- no intermediaries, no university technology transfer office bottleneck. Cross-institutional talent pool: access [[Students]] and [[Companies]] beyond your own university's network.

## Partnership Model

1. **Onboarding** -- Dedicated onboarding with [[Program Directors]] and faculty
2. **Embedding** -- Studyond integrated into thesis or project modules
3. **Matching** -- [[Matching Engine|AI-powered matching]] connects [[Students]] with company [[Topics]], and professors with research opportunities
4. **Scaling** -- Expand across departments and programs with analytics on engagement

The commitment ask is intentionally minimal: one faculty champion, one thesis module, willingness to encourage adoption. No budget, no IT resources, no procurement process.

## Testimonial

## Governance-First Design

Every feature preserves institutional autonomy by default. Faculty approve [[Topics]] before students see them. Academic supervision authority is non-negotiable. Student data stays on the platform; [[Universities]] receive aggregate analytics only. No curriculum changes required. This addresses the core concern: [[Universities]] are governance-first institutions, and any platform touching curricula must enhance [[Academic Governance]], not threaten it.
