# Studyond

Studyond is a Swiss government-backed [[Three-Sided Marketplace]] connecting [[Students]], [[Companies]], and [[Universities]] around thesis topics, research projects, and talent sourcing. It is the infrastructure layer where academia-industry collaboration happens.

## What It Is

Studyond AG gives companies one access point to 20+ Swiss universities for innovation exploration and early talent engagement. The platform turns thesis projects into structured exploration sprints and evidence-based hiring pipelines -- replacing costly pilots, consultants, and career fairs with a single, always-on system.

Founded in 2023 as a University of St. Gallen (HSG) spin-off, Studyond is an ETH startup backed by Innosuisse (Switzerland's federal innovation agency), in partnership with ETH Zurich, HSG, OST, and the Swiss Employers Association. Based in St. Gallen, Switzerland.

## The Core Idea

Three structural needs converge on the thesis process:

- **[[Companies]]** need talent and innovation but lack structured access to universities
- **[[Students]]** need career-relevant thesis topics and industry pathways
- **[[Universities]]** need scalable industry linkage without surrendering academic control

Studyond is the infrastructure layer connecting all three around a single workflow: the thesis project.

## Business Model

The platform is entirely free for [[Students]] and [[Universities]]. Revenue comes from [[Companies]] through [[Subscription Tiers]] -- companies pay to post topics, source talent, and access research capacity across 1,680+ study programs through one login.
